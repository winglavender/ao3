AO3 Year in Review

This webapp downloads the data from your Archive Of Our Own history for a particular year and produces a summary of what you read (total words read, most visited fics, most common ships and tags, etc). It also generates a spreadsheet that you can download to see your reading history in more detail.

IMPORTANT NOTE! If your 2021-2020 reading history exceeds a couple hundred pages, the app may time out before finishing your results. This limitation exists so that the app doesn't overwhelm AO3 with requests and everyone has a chance to request their results.
